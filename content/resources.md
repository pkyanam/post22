# Resources

A curated list of tools and links, grouped by category.

These are selected for quality, not quantity. Everything here is either free or worth paying for.

---

## Learning

**Programming**
- [The Odin Project](https://www.theodinproject.com/) — free full-stack web curriculum
- [CS50](https://cs50.harvard.edu/x/) — Harvard's free intro to computer science
- [freeCodeCamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/) — free, project-based
- [roadmap.sh](https://roadmap.sh/) — learning roadmaps for tech careers

**General Skills**
- [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/) — university courses, many auditable for free
- [MIT OpenCourseWare](https://ocw.mit.edu/) — free MIT course materials
- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/) — free, rigorous, any level
- [DeepLearning.AI](https://www.deeplearning.ai/short-courses/) — free short AI/ML courses

**Books**
- [Atomic Habits — James Clear](https://jamesclear.com/atomic-habits)
- [Deep Work — Cal Newport](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/25744928-deep-work)
- [On Writing Well — William Zinsser](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/53343.On_Writing_Well)
- [So Good They Can't Ignore You — Cal Newport](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/13525945-so-good-they-can-t-ignore-you)

## Productivity

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) — notes, tasks, and wikis — free tier generous
- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) — local markdown notes — free for personal use
- [Todoist](https://todoist.com/) — simple task manager — solid free tier
- [Focusmate](https://www.focusmate.com/) — virtual coworking, great for accountability
- [Forest](https://www.forestapp.cc/) — focus timer app

## Mental Health

- [Open Path Collective](https://openpathcollective.org/) — affordable therapy ($30–$80/session)
- [7 Cups](https://www.7cups.com/) — free peer support and community
- [Headspace](https://www.headspace.com/) — meditation app, discounted for those in financial hardship
- [Woebot](https://woebothealth.com/) — free CBT-based chat tool
- [Feeling Good — David Burns](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/46674.Feeling_Good) — book on CBT

## Networking

- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/) — still the most useful professional network
- [Lunchclub](https://lunchclub.com/) — AI-matched 1:1 meetings
- [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/) — local events in your field
- [ADPList](https://adplist.org/) — free mentorship from professionals

## Finance

- [r/personalfinance wiki](https://www.reddit.com/r/personalfinance/wiki/index/) — comprehensive personal finance guide
- [NerdWallet](https://www.nerdwallet.com/) — comparisons for financial products
- [YNAB](https://www.youneedabudget.com/) — best budgeting app (paid, but worth it)
- [Mint](https://mint.intuit.com/) — free budgeting and tracking
