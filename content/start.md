# Start Here

You graduated. The structure that guided your life for 16+ years is gone.

No schedule handed to you. No clear next step. No one telling you what matters.

post22 is a simple framework to help you rebuild direction on your own terms.

---

## The Framework

There are four steps. They're not a checklist — they overlap. But having a sequence helps.

### 1. Stabilize

Before anything else, get your fundamentals in order.

Sleep. Movement. Mental health. Basic routines. Without these, everything else is harder.

[Go to Stabilize →](/stabilize)

### 2. Explore Careers

Figure out what kind of work you want to do — or at least narrow it down.

Talk to people. Read. Try things. You don't need a final answer, just a direction.

[Go to Career →](/career)

### 3. Build Skills

Pick one or two skills that match your direction and go deep.

Free resources exist for almost everything. Use them.

[Go to Skills →](/skills)

### 4. Generate Income

Get paid. Freelance, contract, or apply for jobs. Build a portfolio. Start small.

[Go to Income →](/income)
